Mariah and Jeff

Posted on 22 July 2017

Cole and I really loved spending our last wedding of the year with Mariah and Jeff. We had so much fun during their Sunset Cliffs engagement shoot we knew the wedding day would be no different. We started our day with the ladies at a beautiful and charming home in Coronado. The gals were all so sweet and relaxed it really made for a lovely afternoon. Once Cole returned from hanging with the guys at a nearby motel Mariah and Jeff were ready or their first look. From the moment Jeff saw his bride he was smitten. For the rest of the day into the evening I kept seeing them steal sweet kisses from one another and whispering how much they loved each other and how happy they were to be married. It really was amazing. Mariah and Jeff you are such kind individuals and it was truly an honor experiencing the love you share right before our very eyes. I think you two have a connection admired by many and see that connection only strengthening as you start this new chapter. Enjoy the photos!
